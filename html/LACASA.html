La Casa De papel

A mysterious man who calls himself "the Professor", together with eight specialists (named after cities) organizes the largest robbery in Spanish history. Together they want to infiltrate the Royal Mint of Spain to print banknotes themselves. The goal is to leave the building with a total of 2.4 billion euros in the bag. They need access to the money printing machines for eleven days. This requires a sophisticated plan to deceive the special forces of the Spanish police. The story is mainly focused on one of the robbers, Tokyo (Úrsula Corberó), because they fight with hostages from within and the police from outside.
